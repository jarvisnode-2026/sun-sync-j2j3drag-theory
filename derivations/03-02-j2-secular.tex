\section{Standard J2 Secular Rates}

From time-averaging $R_{J2}$ (Phase 1):

\begin{align}
\left\langle\frac{d\Omega}{dt}\right\rangle_{J2} &= -\frac{3}{2}\frac{n J_2 R_E^2}{p^2}\cos i\\
\left\langle\frac{d\omega}{dt}\right\rangle_{J2} &= \frac{3}{4}\frac{n J_2 R_E^2}{p^2}(5\cos^2 i - 1)
\end{align}

where $p = a(1-e^2)$, $n = \sqrt{\mu/a^3}$.

For circular orbits ($p = a$):
\begin{align}
\dot{\Omega}_{J2} &= -\frac{3}{2}\frac{n J_2 R_E^2}{a^2}\cos i\\
\dot{\omega}_{J2} &= \frac{3}{4}\frac{n J_2 R_E^2}{a^2}(5\cos^2 i - 1)
\end{align}

\section{Verification}
Matches Vallado (2013) Eq. 9-42, 9-43 exactly. ✓
