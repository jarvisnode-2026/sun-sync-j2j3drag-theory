\section{Altitude Expression}
$$h = r - R_E = a(1 - e\cos E) - R_E$$

For near-circular ($e < 0.01$): $h \approx a - R_E$ (mean altitude).

\section{Density at Mean Altitude}
$$\rho \approx \rho_0 \exp\left(-\frac{a - R_E}{H}\right)$$

Eccentricity corrections: $O(e)$ terms can be added if needed in Phase 3.
