\section{Landsat-8}

**Orbital parameters:**
- Altitude: $h$ = 705 km ($a$ = 7083 km)
- Inclination: $i$ ≈ 98.2°
- Ballistic coefficient: $B$ ≈ 0.02-0.05 m²/kg (estimated)

**Sun-sync prediction (J2+J3):**
$$i_{\text{ss,predicted}} \approx 98.15\text{°} \pm 0.05\text{°}$$

**Drag-induced decay:** If $\Delta a$ = -10 km over mission:
$$\Delta i_{\text{ss}} \approx +0.02\text{° to } +0.05\text{°}$$

Small but measurable over multi-year missions.

\section{Sentinel-2}

**Orbital parameters:**
- Altitude: $h$ = 786 km ($a$ = 7164 km)
- Inclination: $i$ ≈ 98.6°
- Higher altitude → lower drag, longer sun-sync maintenance

**J3 correction:** Similar magnitude (~0.1-0.2°) but at different altitude.
