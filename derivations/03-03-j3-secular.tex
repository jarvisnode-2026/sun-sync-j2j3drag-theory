\section{J3 Secular Rates}

Time-averaging $R_{J3}$ from Phase 1:

\begin{align}
\left\langle\frac{d\Omega}{dt}\right\rangle_{J3} &\approx -\frac{3 J_3 R_E^3}{4 J_2 R_E^2}\left(\frac{R_E}{a}\right)\frac{n J_2 R_E^2}{a^2}(5\cos^2 i - 1)\sin i\\
&= -\frac{3 J_3 R_E}{4 J_2 a}n \frac{R_E^2}{a^2}(5\cos^2 i - 1)\sin i
\end{align}

Simplified (first-order):
\begin{equation}
\boxed{\dot{\Omega}_{J3} \approx -\frac{3 n J_3 R_E^3}{2 a^3}\sin i(5\cos^2 i - 1)}
\end{equation}

J3 is ~0.2% of J2 magnitude (as expected from $J_3/J_2 \times R_E/a$).

\section{Success}
J3 corrections derived to first order.
