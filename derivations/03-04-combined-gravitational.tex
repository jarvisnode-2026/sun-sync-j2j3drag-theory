\section{Complete Gravitational Secular Rates}

Superposition of J2 and J3:

\begin{align}
\boxed{\dot{\Omega}_{\text{grav}} = \dot{\Omega}_{J2} + \dot{\Omega}_{J3}}\\
\boxed{\dot{\omega}_{\text{grav}} = \dot{\omega}_{J2} + \dot{\omega}_{J3}}
\end{align}

Explicitly:
\begin{align}
\dot{\Omega}_{\text{grav}} &= -\frac{3 n J_2 R_E^2}{2 a^2}\cos i - \frac{3 n J_3 R_E^3}{2 a^3}\sin i(5\cos^2 i - 1)\\
\dot{\omega}_{\text{grav}} &= \frac{3 n J_2 R_E^2}{4 a^2}(5\cos^2 i - 1) + (\text{J3 corrections})
\end{align}
